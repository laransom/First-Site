Lee Ransom for Launch Academy!!

I was sitting in my office while working as a Mutual Fund Accountant when I imagined my life as a story. After about two minutes I came to the unfortunate conclusion that it would be a harrowing bore. I considered my favorite stories and realized that what my story was missing was an adventure. So I stood up, walked out of the office, hopped on my bicycle and rode it south until I reached Atlanta. (Disclaimer: Events described in this anecdote have been condensed to improve narrative.)

This action set in motion a period in my life where I would work and live in Costa Rica and Australia for extended periods of time, travel through Europe, Central America and Asia and work for several outfits as a Tour Guide in the United States. So my story has improved from harrowing bore to decidedly mediocre and I couldn't be more satisfied!

So what does this have to do with the Lee Ransom for Launch Academy!! campaign. Well, these experiences included a lot of problem solving that may not seem apparent at first. Finding a job and lodging in a foreign country can be difficult, and even traveling in some countries requires a certain degree of creative problem solving. Working as a Tour Guide I've been faced with problems such as an injured cyclist in need of medical attention that need to be solved quickly and calmly. I believe this kind of real world problem solving is just as useful as the more analytic problem solving that coding requires.

I have chosen Launch Academy because I feel I have the creative/analytic balance that will serve me well in a programming career. Furthermore, I know I learn much more effectively in a group environment than on my own. I can bring my balanced mindset and enthusiasm to the Launch Academy and help encourage a fantastic learning environment for my peers and the instructors. Join the Lee Ransom for Launch Academy!! campaign today and lets pen another chapter to this adventure story.
